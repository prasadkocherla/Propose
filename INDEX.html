My Dearest

As Promise Day dawns upon us, I am very grateful to have you in my life. With each passing moment, my love for you increases and my dedication to you becomes stronger.
I promise to be at your side through everything, to support your dreams, and to cherish our time together. I swear to be your rock, confidant, and greatest friend. I promise to listen, understand, and love you absolutely. I promise to share your happiness as well as your sadness. I will always respect, honour, and be honest with you. But, above all, I swear to love you madly and deeply, now and forever. You are my light, love, and everything, and I promise to spend the rest of my life making you happy. Happy Promise Day to you, my darling. Here's to a lifetime of love, laughter, and countless promises kept.
